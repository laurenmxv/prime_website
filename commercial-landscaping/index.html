What's Included

Full-Scope Commercial Landscaping Services

Every commercial maintenance contract can be built from our full service menu. We scope what your property actually needs — not a pre-packaged bundle.

Lawn Mowing & Edging

Scheduled mowing, string trimming, edging along sidewalks and curbs, and blowing of all hard surfaces. Frequency customized to your property.

Shrub & Hedge Trimming

Shaping, pruning, and maintenance of all shrubs, hedges, and ornamental plantings to maintain a clean, professional appearance year-round.

Irrigation Management

Scheduled irrigation audits, head adjustments, zone checks, and seasonal programming. We identify leaks and inefficiencies before they become expensive.

Seasonal Color Rotations

Annual and seasonal color plantings for entrances, courtyards, and feature beds. Planned to keep your property looking fresh every quarter.

Mulching & Bed Maintenance

Fresh mulch installation, bed edging, weed control, and plant health monitoring across all landscape beds on the property.

Tree & Palm Care

Trimming, shaping, and health monitoring for palms, ornamental trees, and shade trees. Includes hurricane prep trimming in advance of storm season.

Seasonal Cleanups

Post-storm cleanup, seasonal leaf and debris removal, and annual comprehensive deep cleanups to reset your property's appearance.

Digital Service Reporting

After every commercial visit, you receive a timestamped digital report with photos, tasks completed, and any flagged issues requiring attention.

Sustainable Practices

Florida-native plant recommendations, water-efficient irrigation programming, and organic fertilization options to reduce your property's environmental footprint.

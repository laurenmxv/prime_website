About Us

Last updated: April 2026

Orlando's Commercial Landscaping Experts — Built From the Ground Up

Founded in 2025, Prime Outdoor Experts started with one crew, one truck, and one goal: to give Orlando commercial properties the consistent, professional landscaping they deserve.

Our Story

Why We Started — And Why We're Still Here

Prime Outdoor Experts was founded on a simple observation: Orlando's commercial properties cycle through landscaping vendors that overpromise, underdeliver, and send a different crew every week. The vision for a different kind of company was clear from day one.

The idea was simple: keep the same crew assigned to the same properties, equip them with the right tools, and actually communicate with clients. We don't have a call center — you talk to the people who manage your property.

We're independently owned and operated. That's not just a tagline. It's how we run every day.

Our Values

What We Stand For

Reliability Over Everything

We show up. We show up on the right day, with the right crew, doing the right work. Reliability is the foundation — everything else is built on top of it. If we ever fall short, you hear from us first.

Transparent Communication

No surprises. If your irrigation system needs a $400 repair, we tell you before we do it. If we see a problem developing in your beds, we document it and flag it. You're always informed before you're billed.

Pride in the Work

Our crews take personal pride in the properties they maintain. We don't rush through jobs to hit a daily quota. We do the work right, and we notice the details that matter — because your property's appearance reflects on us too.

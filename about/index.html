Orlando's Commercial Landscaping Experts — Built From the Ground Up

Founded in 2015, Prime Outdoor Experts started with one crew, one truck, and one goal: to give Orlando commercial properties the consistent, professional landscaping they deserve.

Our Story

Why We Started — And Why We're Still Here

Prime Outdoor Experts was founded in 2015 by [OWNER NAME — TODO] after years working in Orlando's commercial property management industry. After watching property after property cycle through landscaping vendors — each one overpromising, underdelivering, and sending a different crew every week — the vision for a different kind of company became clear.

The idea was simple: hire the best crews, keep them assigned to the same properties, equip them with the right tools, and actually communicate with clients. A decade later, that idea has grown into a team serving over 200 commercial and residential properties across Central Florida's most competitive real estate markets.

We're still independently owned and operated. We don't have a call center — you talk to the people who manage your property. That's not just a tagline. It's how we run every day.

Our Values

What We Stand For

Reliability Over Everything

We show up. We show up on the right day, with the right crew, doing the right work. Reliability is the foundation — everything else is built on top of it. If we ever fall short, you hear from us first.

Transparent Communication

No surprises. If your irrigation system needs a $400 repair, we tell you before we do it. If we see a problem developing in your beds, we document it and flag it. You're always informed before you're billed.

Pride in the Work

Our crews take personal pride in the properties they maintain. We don't rush through jobs to hit a daily quota. We do the work right, and we notice the details that matter — because your property's appearance reflects on us too.

Lake Mary Property & Landscape Notes

The Heathrow office park is arguably Seminole County's most recognizable commercial address. Companies including AAA, LexisNexis, and dozens of financial services and technology firms have regional headquarters here. The landscaping on these campuses is a direct extension of each company's brand — it's the first thing clients and employees see. Our commercial teams understand this. We don't simply mow and go; we manage the full visual experience of a property's exterior, from the boulevard entry to the building foundation beds to the parking lot tree wells.

Colonial TownPark adds a retail and mixed-use dimension to Lake Mary's commercial landscape. The open-air environment means landscaping is part of the guest experience, not just a backdrop. Our crews approach these properties with that in mind — clean lines, fresh color, and nothing left looking half-done when customers are walking by.

On the residential side, Heathrow Golf & Country Club sets the benchmark. The community's HOA documents are detailed about grass species, mulch colors, and approved plant material, and properties within Heathrow are inspected regularly. We've worked within these frameworks for years and know how to deliver landscaping that passes inspection, impresses neighbors, and holds up through the heat and rain cycles that define Central Florida summers.

Beyond Heathrow, communities like Timacuan and Magnolia Plantation bring similar expectations. These are neighborhoods where residents have invested significantly in their homes and take exterior appearance seriously. Whether you're a homeowner wanting flawless weekly maintenance or a property manager overseeing dozens of units, we bring the same level of professional service — on schedule, every visit, with no shortcuts on the detail work.

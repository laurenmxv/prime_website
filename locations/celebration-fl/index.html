Celebration Landscaping Services

Our Services in Celebration

Celebration is unlike any other community in Central Florida. Disney developed it in 1994 with a specific vision — New Urbanist architecture, walkable streets, beautifully maintained common areas, and a community aesthetic that depends on every property owner holding up their end of the streetscape. The HOA covenants in Celebration specify everything from approved grass types to permitted plant species to mulch colors, and the association actively inspects and enforces those standards. For landscaping providers, this means you either know the rules and deliver accordingly — or you don't last long. We know the rules, and our Celebration crews are trained to work within this framework precisely.

HOA-Compliant Lawn Maintenance

Precise mowing heights, clean edge lines, and approved trimming practices for Celebration's grass specifications. We document our visits and are available for HOA audit inquiries.

Approved Plant Installation

Landscape renovations and new installations using plant palettes approved for Celebration's aesthetic guidelines. We work from the community's approved plant list and coordinate with HOA review when required.

Commercial & Mixed-Use Properties

Storefronts, restaurants, and mixed-use properties along Celebration Avenue and the downtown district. Clean, curated landscaping that matches the community's premium character.

Irrigation Systems

System installation, repairs, and controller calibration. Celebration's streetscape irrigation is complex — we understand both community systems and individual property setups throughout the villages.

Mulching & Bed Maintenance

Seasonal mulch applications in approved colors, bed edging, and ground cover maintenance. Fresh, uniform beds are one of the most visible indicators of HOA compliance in Celebration.

Fertilization & Weed Suppression

Seasonal programs that keep turf dense and weed-free — essential in a community where every lawn is visible and neighbors take notice. We apply targeted treatments that protect plant material while controlling weeds.

Celebration Property & Landscape Notes

Celebration's residential villages — West Village, North Village, South Village, and the areas around Artisan Park and Roseville — each have slightly different streetscape characters, but all share the community's overarching commitment to architectural and landscape quality. Homes here are close together on relatively small lots, which means every detail of exterior maintenance is on full display to neighbors and passersby. An unedged lawn, an overgrown shrub, or a mulch bed that's gone thin and weedy gets noticed fast. Our crews are trained to catch these details before the HOA does.

The community's approved plant palette is more curated than most HOAs in Osceola County. Certain invasive species that might slip through unnoticed in other neighborhoods are explicitly prohibited in Celebration, and there are specific requirements around foundation plantings, tree heights near structures, and seasonal color placements. We stay current with Celebration's community standards and update our practices when the guidelines are revised.

Downtown Celebration along Celebration Avenue is the commercial heart of the community. The restaurants, shops, and mixed-use buildings that line the lakefront and the town square depend on immaculate exterior presentation — Celebration attracts visitors from outside the community who come specifically for the ambiance, and that ambiance is only preserved when every property owner treats their exterior as a public asset. We serve several commercial properties in the downtown and understand the visual expectations that come with this address.

Because Celebration is near Walt Disney World and the broader tourist corridor, the community maintains a noticeably higher baseline of landscape quality than most residential areas in Central Florida. That's the baseline we work from — not the minimum required, but the standard the community actually operates at. For homeowners who want their property to stand out even within Celebration's high standards, we also offer premium landscape design services and seasonal enhancement programs.

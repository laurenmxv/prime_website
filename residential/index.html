Why Homeowners Choose Us

Lawn care that actually shows up — every week.

The biggest frustration homeowners have with landscaping companies isn't the work. It's the no-shows, the rotating crews, and the silence between visits. Prime Outdoor Experts is built to fix all three.

We currently service 35+ residential properties weekly across Orange and Seminole counties — single-family homes, lakefront properties, townhomes, and high-end residential developments. Every property gets the same crew on the same day each week. Your gate code, irrigation quirks, pet considerations, and aesthetic preferences are documented and remembered visit-to-visit.

After every service, you get a digital report — the same kind of photo-documented receipt our commercial clients receive — sent to your phone or email. You always know what was done on your property, even when you're not home.

For homeowners who've cycled through two or three landscapers (most have), the consistency is what stands out. We don't ghost. We don't bill add-ons separately for routine work. We don't show up at 7 AM on a Sunday with a leaf blower. The crew, the schedule, and the scope are predictable on purpose.

Service Tiers

Three residential service tiers.

Most Orlando homeowners fit into one of three patterns. Pricing is transparent — quoted per property after a free walk-through.

Tier 1

Weekly Lawn Maintenance

Mow, edge, trim, blow off hardscapes. Weekly during the growing season (April–October), bi-weekly the rest of the year. The most common residential package — works for typical quarter-acre lots.

Typical range: $35–$80 per visit

Tier 2

Full-Service Property Care

Tier 1 plus monthly hedge shaping, quarterly bed maintenance, seasonal mulch refresh, irrigation walk-throughs, and seasonal color rotations. Designed for homes where curb appeal matters and the homeowner doesn't want to manage multiple vendors.

Typical range: $180–$450 per month

Tier 3

Estate-Level Care

Tier 2 plus tree and palm management, hurricane prep, irrigation programming, hardscape coordination, lighting maintenance, and on-call response for events or special tour days. Designed for larger lots, lakefront properties, and homes with detailed landscape design.

Typical range: $500–$1,500+ per month

All tiers include the same crew assignment, photo-documented service reports, and direct line to the owner. No call center, no rotating crews.

What to Expect

From first quote to first visit.

Free walk-through (within 2 business hours)

We respond to every quote request within two business hours. We schedule a free walk-through at your convenience — usually within 2–3 days. The walk takes 15–20 minutes and lets us understand your property, your standards, and any specific concerns (irrigation issues, neglected beds, problem trees, neighbor considerations).

Written quote within 24 hours

You get a written quote by email within one business day after the walk-through. The quote itemizes scope (what's included, what's not), frequency, and pricing. No upsell, no high-pressure callbacks. Take your time deciding.

First service within one week of approval

Once you approve the quote, we schedule your first visit within a week — usually sooner. The first visit is the cleanup visit: edges, weeds, trims, mulch refresh if needed. After that, your service falls into the regular weekly or bi-weekly rhythm.

Same crew, every visit

Your property is assigned to a specific crew. They learn your gate code, your dog's name, your irrigation controller, your trash pickup day. The same team shows up every week — different equipment days, but the same people. If we ever need to send a backup crew (rare — maybe for crew vacation), we tell you in advance.

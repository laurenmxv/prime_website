Lawn Care

Lawn Care in Orlando, FL

What's Included

Full-Service Lawn Maintenance

Every visit from our crews covers the complete picture — not just a quick mow and go. We treat every property the way we'd want our own lawn treated: thorough, consistent, and done right the first time.

  • Mowing

    We mow at the correct height for your specific grass type — every turf variety has an ideal range, and cutting too short invites weeds and stress. We alternate mowing patterns on each visit to prevent ruts and encourage upright growth, and we never scalp during the dry season when turf is already stressed.

  • Edging

    Crisp, defined edges along every driveway, sidewalk, curb, and landscape bed. Sharp edges are one of the biggest visual upgrades a lawn can get — they signal a well-maintained property to guests, tenants, and HOA inspectors alike. We use steel-blade edgers for hard borders and string trimmers for curved beds.

  • String Trimming

    Every obstacle the mower can't reach — tree bases, fence lines, irrigation heads, light poles, and landscape borders — gets careful string-trimmer attention. We're precise enough to trim around sprinkler heads without clipping the nozzles, which matters more than most people realize until a head gets damaged.

  • Clipping & Debris Cleanup

    All grass clippings are blown off hard surfaces — driveways, parking areas, entry walks, and patios — so you're not tracking green back inside. During rainy season (June–September) when grass grows fast and clippings are heavy, we double-pass areas prone to accumulation. We leave the property looking finished, not just cut.

  • Seasonal Fertilization (Add-On)

    Central Florida's sandy soils drain nutrients quickly, and the alkaline pH requires the right formulation. We apply slow-release fertilizer on a seasonal schedule calibrated to your grass type — typically four applications per year. Proper feeding during the spring green-up and fall transition keeps your turf thick enough to naturally resist weeds.

  • Flexible Scheduling

    Weekly service during summer's fast-growing rainy season, bi-weekly during the slower dry months — or we can build a custom rotation based on your property's actual growth rate. We assign the same crew to your property whenever possible so they know your layout, your preferences, and where that one buried irrigation head is hiding.

How We Work

Our Lawn Care Process

Getting started is simple. We keep the process straightforward so you're not dealing with paperwork and callbacks when all you want is a reliable lawn crew.

01

Free On-Site Assessment

We walk your property with you (or on our own for commercial clients) to measure square footage, identify your grass type, note any problem areas like poor drainage or thin turf, and check your existing irrigation coverage. This takes 20–30 minutes and costs nothing. We don't quote over the phone because every lawn is different.

02

Custom Maintenance Plan

Based on your property and goals, we put together a written maintenance schedule: visit frequency, what's included at each visit, and any recommended add-ons like fertilization or weed control. Commercial clients get a service agreement with defined scope, so there are no surprises at invoice time.

03

Consistent, Scheduled Service

Your crew arrives on schedule — same day of the week, same team. After each visit, commercial clients receive a digital completion log. If we ever spot something that needs attention between visits (a broken irrigation head, a new patch of chinch bug damage, a tree limb hanging over your roof), we flag it proactively rather than waiting to be asked.

Why Orlando Property Owners Choose Prime Outdoor Experts

We Know Florida Turf

St. Augustine grass dominates Central Florida lawns for good reason — it's heat-tolerant, shade-adaptable, and handles foot traffic. But it's also susceptible to chinch bugs, gray leaf spot, and take-all root rot when mowed too short or over-irrigated. We've been maintaining Central Florida turf since 2025 and understand the seasonal rhythms that separate a great-looking lawn from a struggling one.

Year-Round Reliability

Unlike northern landscaping companies that go quiet in winter, Orlando lawns need attention every single month of the year. Our crews work 52 weeks — adjusting frequency and service scope by season rather than disappearing after the first cold snap. When rainy season hits in June and your St. Augustine shoots up two inches a week, we're already scheduled and ready.

Commercial-Grade for Every Client

We maintain properties across Central Florida. The equipment, the systems, and the professionalism we bring to an HOA entrance or office park are the same standards we apply to every residential client. You get a licensed, insured company with real infrastructure — not a one-truck operation that disappears when things get busy.
